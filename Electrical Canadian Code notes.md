
Baseboard heaters :
- Rule of thumb : 10W/sq. Ex : Room is 247sq * 10 = 2470W of heat needed

Receptacle:
- 26-724
	- No point along the wall can there be more than1.8 meters (6 feet) from a receptacle. In other words, there can be at most 3.6m (12 feet) between two receptacles
	- 900m (3') => usable wall space
	- 4.5m (15') => hallway
- Maximum of 12 receptacle per circuit
